About Me

Gitau Harrison

Background

I quit school as as soon as I completed my high school diploma. Strangely, I became very bored with normal school life when I was 14 years of age, even though I was an average performer when compared aganist the elite students of Friends School Kamusinga back in the day. I have never been as depressed, hopeless and with no sense of purpose and direction as during my tail years of school.

However, I found a dimming ray of interest whenever an acquintance of my came to our campus dormitory after a Computer Science class. I would ask with little interest what he did and he would tell (not show) me bits of his incomplete work because, well, I didn't know anything about Computer Science. But this was enough to make me realize that I had a tiny some interest in the creative aspect of what he was learning. Technically speaking, I have not been to campus because there was nothing I did within my initial 6 months stay there.

I friend's business ambition got me up and running to learn how to program so I could be of value to his idea. And that is where my journey began.

My Location

Currently, I am based in Nairobi, Kenya. This is my home city. I consider Seoul, South Korea, my city as well because it was there that I actively began to work on developing production software for Tinker Educaton, a branch of EMCAST.

My Interests

I blog about programming, physical computing, capentry, musical instruments and fitness and wellness. Only these do I engage myself in 100% of the time. I cannot hold any conversation outside these listed items.

I have no other social media presence other than the necessary work sites such as GitHub and local programming communities near me. And now that I have this blog, this will be my find-me-here-any-time social media platform of choice.

My Gratitude

Thank you for stopping by! I hope you find something of interest. If you like something or you are interested in clarification of the contents blogged here, I would be glad to hear from you in the comments sections.
